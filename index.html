Design Goals

  1. Innovation at the speed of light for FREE
  2. limitless scalability: online, offline or a road in between the two
  3. Simple design: highly modular, ease of use
  4. Secure design
  5. Stability: little or no regression, update regression
  6. Zero server configurability: we don't configure your server, just install the software and you have all the features
  7. OS agnostic
  8. Parallel run: you can do parallel run while ignoring us
  9. Full documentation
  10. Reach: smoothen your internal process, reaching the unreacheable, satisfy your current client
  11. Speed: no need for end of day processes: everything computed on the fly, information are available at your finger tips.
  12. Integrated yet modularized: take only what you want
  13. Automated disaster recovery
